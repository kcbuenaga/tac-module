# APA Citation Format Guide (7th Edition)

**Purpose:** American Psychological Association citation standards

---

## Reference List Format

### Journal Article

**Format:**
```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. Title of Journal, Volume(Issue), page range. https://doi.org/xxx
```

**Example:**
```
Silva, J. C., & Santos, M. A. (2023). Digital transformation in Brazilian small businesses. Journal of Contemporary Management, 25(3), 145-162. https://doi.org/10.1234/jcm.2023.123
```

### Book

**Format:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of book (Edition). Publisher.
```

**Example:**
```
Kotler, P., & Keller, K. L. (2021). Marketing management (15th ed.). Pearson Education.
```

### Book Chapter

**Format:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of chapter. In B. B. Editor (Ed.), Title of book (pp. page range). Publisher.
```

**Example:**
```
Santos, M. (2022). Service innovation. In P. Oliveira (Ed.), Modern management (pp. 78-95). Atlas Publishing.
```

### Thesis/Dissertation

**Format:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of dissertation [Doctoral dissertation, Institution]. Database or Archive.
```

**Example:**
```
Almeida, A. P. (2023). Digital marketing strategies in Brazilian retail [Doctoral dissertation, Fundação Getulio Vargas]. FGV Repository.
```

### Online Source

**Format:**
```
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Website Name. URL
```

**Example:**
```
SEBRAE. (2024, January 15). How to digitally transform your business. SEBRAE Portal. https://sebrae.com.br/transformacao-digital
```

## In-Text Citation

### Direct Quote

**Format:** (Author, Year, p. Page)

**Example:**
> According to Silva (2023), "digital transformation is essential for the competitiveness of Brazilian SMEs" (p. 47).

### Paraphrase

**Format:** (Author, Year)

**Example:**
> Digital transformation has become fundamental for small businesses (Silva, 2023).

### Multiple Authors

- **1 author:** (Silva, 2023)
- **2 authors:** (Silva & Santos, 2023)
- **3+ authors:** (Silva et al., 2023)

### Multiple Works

**Same author, same year:**
- (Silva, 2023a, 2023b)

**Multiple authors:**
- (Santos, 2022; Silva, 2023)

## Reference List Format Rules

- **Order:** Alphabetical by first author's surname
- **Author names:** Surname, Initial(s)
- **Capitalization:** Only first word of title and subtitle, proper nouns
- **Journal titles:** Capitalize all major words, italicize
- **Indentation:** Hanging indent (first line flush, subsequent lines indented)
- **Spacing:** Double-spaced throughout

---

## Common Patterns

### DOI Formatting
- Always include DOI when available
- Format as: https://doi.org/10.xxxx/xxxxx
- No period after DOI

### Multiple Authors
- Use & before last author
- Up to 20 authors: list all
- 21+ authors: list first 19, then "...", then final author

### No Author
- Start with title, move year after title
- Use title (shortened) in in-text citation

### No Date
- Use (n.d.) in place of year

## APA vs ABNT Quick Reference

| Element | APA | ABNT |
|---------|-----|------|
| Author name | Surname, Initial | SOBRENOME, Nome |
| Title case | Sentence case | Title case |
| Journal | Italicized | Not italicized |
| Location | Not required | Required |
| Access date | Not required | Required for web |
| Quote format | (Author, Year, p. #) | (AUTOR, ano, p. #) |
