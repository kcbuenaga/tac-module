# Step 03: Motivation Boost

**Goal:** Celebrate progress, provide perspective, and suggest next actionable steps to keep momentum going.

---

## 1. Analyze Progress Level

Based on data from step-01, determine progress stage:

**Stage 1: Getting Started (0-2,000 words)**
- Just beginning
- Focus on establishing routine
- Encourage small wins

**Stage 2: Early Progress (2,000-8,000 words)**
- Building momentum
- Celebrate consistency
- Keep the habit going

**Stage 3: Mid-Journey (8,000-15,000 words)**
- Significant progress made
- Acknowledge the grind
- Remind them they're over halfway

**Stage 4: Approaching Completion (15,000-22,000 words)**
- Final push
- Celebrate how far they've come
- Focus on finishing strong

**Stage 5: Final Stretch (22,000+ words)**
- Almost there!
- Defense preparation focus
- Victory is near
